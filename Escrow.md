# ESCROW IPO

## The proposal
Please **vote Yes** if you want Qubic Capital to bid on ESCROW IPO. In that case, please also enter the amount you want to allocate from existing funds. 

**Vote No** if you do not want Qubic Capital to bid at all. 

The final amount that Qubic Capital will bid will be calculated as the weighted average among Yes votes. 

To know more about how IPO proposals work on Qvault, visit <https://hermantoothrot10.github.io/qubic-capital-manual/proposals/ipop/>
