# Qubic Capital Voting: QIP Proposal

**Date:** 28 March 2026  
**Scope:** Shareholder Vote on Dev Fund & SC Holder Dividends

*Qubic Capital representatives pov*
---

## Background

The community is currently reviewing three proposals regarding the allocation of development funds and Smart Contract (SC) holder dividends:

- **Proposal I:** 2% dev fund, SC holder dividends reduced to 3%, ICO creation split 40% dev / 60% holders  
- **Proposal II:** 1% dev fund, SC holder dividends 2%, ICO creation split 33.3% dev / 66.6% holders  
- **Proposal III:** 2% dev fund, SC holder dividends 5%, ICO creation split 29% dev / 71% holders (not recommended)  

After internal analysis, Proposal III is excluded from consideration due to its high split limiting ICO volume.

---

## Analysis

### Dev Fund
- Proposal I: 2% of revenue  
- Proposal II: 1% of revenue  
- Recommendation: Proposal II ensures sufficient dev support while keeping operational costs lean

### SC Holder Dividends
- Proposal I: 3%  
- Proposal II: 2%  
- Recommendation: Proposal II maintains reasonable dividends while making platform more attractive to new projects

### ICO Creation Split
- Proposal I: 40% dev / 60% holders  
- Proposal II: 33.3% dev / 66.6% holders  
- Recommendation: Proposal II provides a more balanced incentive for both developers and holders

### Long-term Impact
- Proposal I: Keeps SC alive but reduces attractiveness for new projects  
- Proposal II: More attractive for new projects, sustainable revenue growth, maintainable platform  
- Recommendation: Proposal II maximizes long-term platform viability and revenue growth

---

## Recommendation

Qubic Capital representatives recommends accepting Proposal II.

### Voting guidance for shareholders:
- Vote YES for Proposal II  
- Vote NO if considering Proposal I

Proposal II balances platform sustainability, shareholder value, and developer incentives while supporting long-term growth of the Qubic ecosystem.
